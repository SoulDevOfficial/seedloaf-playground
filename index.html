Why was Seedloaf created?

Seedloaf was inspired by what the team at PloudOS did. There are many options for free Minecraft servers, but there are so few which actually work. For some, it is because the hardware provided is not sufficient. For others, it is because the player restrictions are too severe. We aim to provide a service that is both free and functional.

Will I be locked in once I decide to use Seedloaf?

Nope! While we strive to ensure that our service is as easy and efficient to use as possible, we also understand that different users have different requirements. If at any stage you decide that Seedloaf is not the right fit for you, you can download your world data and move to a different provider - no questions asked.

How do you make money?

We don't. Seedloaf is still in the early stages, however we never plan on charging money for our baseline services. What you get now will always be free. We run ads to offset the costs of hosting the platform, and are actively working on introducing premium features in the future.

Where are the servers located?

We currently only have servers in North America. This is to ensure that we can provide the best possible service before we begin expanding into new regions.

If there are regions you would like to see us expand to, please email us at hello@seedloaf.com .
